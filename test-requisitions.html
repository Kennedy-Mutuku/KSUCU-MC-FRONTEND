📋 How to Use the System

  1. For Users:
    • Go to the main homepage (localhost:5173)
    • Look for the Package icon in the Quick Links sidebar on the right
    • Click "Requisitions" to access the form
    • Fill out the form with items needed, quantities, pickup/return times
    • Submit the requisition
  2. For Admins:
    • Go to worship-docket-admin
    • Enter password: Overseer
    • Click the "Manage Requisitions" button
    • View, approve, release, and track all requisitions
    • Download PDF reports and add comments
